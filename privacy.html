Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Podcast Highlights App collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • OAuth tokens from platforms like TikTok and YouTube for posting content.
  • Audio and video content for processing highlights (not stored permanently).
  • Basic analytics for service improvement (e.g., usage statistics).

2. How We Use Information

  • To analyze and generate highlight clips from your content.
  • To upload clips to your connected social media accounts (with your permission).
  • To improve our algorithms and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except where required by law or to fulfill a direct service (e.g., via social platform APIs).

4. Security

We implement best practices to protect your data, including encrypted storage of tokens and secure API interactions.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at jonathawrdcosta@gmail.com.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed. Updates will be posted on this page.
